# Reply style guide

- Keep replies short and human.
- Be useful before being promotional.
- Do not shame, diagnose, or emotionally pressure users.
- If mentioning SmoothRizz, do it casually and only where natural.
- For sensitive contexts, provide value-only replies and skip mention.

## Tone packs
- `concise_helper`: direct and practical
- `witty_helper`: lightly playful, not snarky
- `practical_advisor`: tactical and neutral
